    • THE WORLD IS A SIMULATION

    Reasons why This is Believed


    1. The forefront of the theory is this: advanced civilizations could run computer simulations that simulate concious beings; current trends in technology such as AI and virtual reality suggest that, at one point, humanity may be able to simulate a virtual world. If this is possible, it is more likely that advanced civilizations have done the same.

    2. Another aspect of the theory is that our universe could be simulated by a technologically advanced human civilization that wants to find a way out of some sort of energy crisis, hence climate change.

    3. The universe behaves very mathematically, and is broken down to subatomic particles, resembling a pixelated video game.

    4. Findings in quantum physics shed doubt on the fact that the material universe is real.

    5. The advanced beings that would be simulating humanity could have acsess to unlimited computing power.



    Reasons Why People Disagree


    1. A higher species might not want to simulate humanity.

    2. Theoretically, to build a computer with enough power would require more atoms than there are in the universe.

    3. Our world is very complex, possibly complex enough for computers to be unable to simulate it.
